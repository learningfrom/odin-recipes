Lasagna Cookies

Description

This is an unusal delicacy, combining the best of both worlds: lasagna and cookies!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Chocolate chips
  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Boil noodles
  2. Spread cheese on noodles
  3. Spread tomato sauce on cheese
  4. Mix flour and butter
  5. Beat in eggs
  6. Stir in chocolate chips
  7. Bake mounds of dough for 10 minutes at 350Â° F
  8. Enjoy!
